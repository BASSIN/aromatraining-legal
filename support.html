AromaTraining — Support

AromaTraining is a smell-training app for olfactory rehabilitation (anosmia and parosmia). It works fully offline: no account, no sign-up, all data stays on your device.

Contact

Questions, bug reports, feature ideas: 01kz01@gmail.com. We usually reply within a couple of days.

Frequently asked

How do I move my data to a new phone? Settings → Data → “Save a backup”, store the file (e.g. in iCloud/Files), then on the new device Settings → Data → “Restore from backup”.

I don’t feel anything during training — is it working? That’s expected at first: the first shifts usually appear around weeks 4–6 of daily practice. Consistency matters more than intensity.

Is AromaTraining a medical device? No. It is a supportive wellness tool, not a substitute for medical advice. If your sense of smell is impaired, consult an ENT specialist or neurologist.
